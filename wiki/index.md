## Concepts

### White Paper Series (Papers 1–5)

*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/WP1_thirteen_dimensions_overview|WP1_thirteen_dimensions_overview]] — **Paper 1 of 5.** Executive summary defining all 13 dimensions of cultural mismatch driving Indigenous worker attrition, bridge candidates at a glance, the readiness spectrum, regional variation, and the series roadmap.
*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/WP2_constructive_conflict_cultural_mismatch|WP2_constructive_conflict_cultural_mismatch]] — **Paper 2 of 5.** Demonstrates that CRM (aviation), TPS (Toyota), and Indigenous relational culture converge on the same structural answer for dimensions 1–7 (communication and conflict). Complete temporal architecture from seconds (PACE) to years (TPS consensus).
*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/WP3_smart_scheduling_workforce_fungibility|WP3_smart_scheduling_workforce_fungibility]] — **Paper 3 of 5.** Argues the scheduling collision (dimensions 8–9) is solvable today with AI scheduling, video-based training, AI cross-training, and digital poka-yoke. Documents AS9100D compliance for all proposed tools.
*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/WP4_structural_cultural_barriers|WP4_structural_cultural_barriers]] — **Paper 4 of 5 ("The Research Agenda").** Bridge candidates and research questions for dimensions 10–13 (urgency hierarchy, learning and knowledge validation, geographic rootedness, credential gatekeeping). Connects to the three pilot proposals.
*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/WP5_research_implementation_roadmap|WP5_research_implementation_roadmap]] — **Paper 5 of 5 ("From Theory to Practice").** Two-tier research roadmap: 7 Tier 1 studies (graduate-level, minimal cost) and 6 Tier 2 studies (university-industry consortium pilots). Includes sequencing, institutional homes, and OCAP compliance.
