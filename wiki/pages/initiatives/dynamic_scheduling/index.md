# dynamic_scheduling

The **Dynamic Scheduling Rotation** initiative designs a capacity-pooling scheduling overlay that coordinates peer-to-peer shift rotations. This allows First Nations workers to fulfill community, ceremonial, and seasonal harvesting obligations without sacrificing employment, while ensuring B2B corporate employers receive continuous, auditable operational coverage [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_integration_exchange_system#L22-L22|Source BCI System, §2.4]].

## 2. Inception Context

Rigid, lockstep shift scheduling acts as a major structural barrier and friction point in safety-critical manufacturing and aerospace. Traditional HR policies treat cultural and seasonal commitments as attendance infractions, leading to high turnover and operational alienation [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L17-L17|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §1.1]]. Under this initiative, the platform uses AI-driven capacity aggregation to model job positions as shareable capacity slots, matching qualified, pre-certified candidates in local peer pools to cover shifts dynamically [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L20-L21|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §3.3]].
