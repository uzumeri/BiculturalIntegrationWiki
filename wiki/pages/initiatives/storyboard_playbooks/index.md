# storyboard_playbooks

The **Dual-Register SOP Translation** initiative focuses on auditing corporate Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and rewriting them into paired formats that bridge dry, expository safety specifications with relational, consequence-based storytelling registers [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_integration_exchange_system#L23-L25|Source BCI System, §2.1]].

## 1. Subcollection Map

This initiative's design, operational examples, and risk factors are detailed across the following documents:

*   [[wiki/pages/initiatives/storyboard_playbooks/design|design]] — Prompt schemas, translation structures, and platform configurations for the Cosolvent/CommonContext engine.
*   [[wiki/pages/initiatives/storyboard_playbooks/scenarios|scenarios]] — Narrative worked examples demonstrating how tool control and resource management SOPs are translated relational.
*   [[wiki/pages/initiatives/storyboard_playbooks/risks|risks]] — Risk register documenting compliance gaps, OCAP data sovereignty drift, and quality validation metrics.

---

## 2. Inception Context

Standard industrial SOPs are written in a rules-first expository register (passive voice, abstract steps) that creates severe cognitive bandwidth blocks and alienation on the ground [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L15-L19|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §1.1]]. Under this initiative, the platform uses AI-driven translation overlays to match the technical audit standard with narrative pedagogy based on Indigenous oral traditions, ensuring genuine process internalization [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_integration_exchange_system#L25-L28|Source BCI System, §2.1]].
