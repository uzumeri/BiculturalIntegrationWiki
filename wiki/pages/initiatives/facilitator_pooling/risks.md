# facilitator_pooling_risks

This risk register outlines the operational, cultural, and financial risks associated with the Shared Facilitator Pooling initiative [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_integration_exchange_system#L21-L21|Source BCI System, §2.3]].

## 1. Risk Register Matrix

| Risk ID | Description | Severity | Probability | Mitigation Protocol |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **FR-01** | **Facilitator Burnout**: A high ratio of placements per facilitator leads to delayed responses, missed checkpoints, and poor support quality. | High | High | **Capacity Hard Cap**: The registry software strictly blocks assigning more than 4 active candidate placements to any single facilitator at one time [[wiki/pages/sources/source_manyguns_roadmap#L18-L18|Source Manyguns Roadmap, §3]]. |
| **FR-02** | **Dispute Creep**: Facilitators are pulled into formal legal disputes, union grievances, or human resources contract negotiations outside their scope. | Medium | Medium | **Boundary Protocols**: Facilitators act as relational guides and buffers, not as legal counsel or corporate HR representatives. Platform guidelines define clear escalation limits. |
| **FR-03** | **Honoraria Settlement Lag**: Institutional invoice processing cycles (e.g. university 60-day lag) delay payment, creating economic strain and trust deficits. | High | Medium | **Bridging Fund**: The platform runs an escrow account that pays facilitators within 48 hours of checkpoint validation, reconciling with clients later [[wiki/pages/sources/source_manyguns_roadmap#L21-L21|Source Manyguns Roadmap, §4.1]]. |
| **FR-04** | **Employer Non-Cooperation**: An SME ignores the facilitator's advisory notes, leading to candidate alienation and high turnover. | High | Low | **Readiness Degradation**: The system flags uncooperative supervisors and downgrades their readiness score, restricting their access to future candidate matches. |

---

## 2. Validation Metrics

Prior to expanding the facilitator registry beyond the initial Mount Royal University pilot:
1.  **Time-to-Payment Metric**: 100% of facilitator honoraria must be processed and paid within 48 hours of checkpoint sign-off.
2.  **Facilitator Ratio Compliance**: The average active candidate load per facilitator must remain at or below 3.0.
3.  **Onboarding Retention target**: Placements supported by the facilitator pool must show a 90-day retention rate of at least 80% in ClientSynth simulation validation [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L20-L21|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §3.3]].
