# facilitator_pooling

The **Shared Facilitator Pooling** initiative pools independent community-based Elders, cultural HR specialists, and mentors as a shared virtual service (shadow capacity) to support local companies and candidates. This model distributes the overhead costs of cultural integration across multiple Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) that cannot afford full-time Indigenous relations departments [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_integration_exchange_system#L21-L21|Source BCI System, §2.3]].

## 2. Inception Context

SMEs represent the majority of employers in precision fabrication, manufacturing, and aerospace in Western Canada. While many are eager to meet Indigenous procurement and hiring targets, they face high cognitive bandwidth bottlenecks and lack internal bicultural integration experience [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L22-L23|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §4.1]]. By pooling local cultural liaisons, the platform establishes a scalable support system that monitors placement health, protects candidates, and guides employers without duplicating overhead [[wiki/pages/sources/source_manyguns_roadmap#L28-L28|Source Manyguns Roadmap, §2.3]].
