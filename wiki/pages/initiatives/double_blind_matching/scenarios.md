# double_blind_matching_scenarios

This document records the worked bicultural match cases and progressive disclosure logs used to validate the Cosolvent matching heuristics [[wiki/pages/sources/source_storyboard_competitive_landscape#L22-L22|Source Competitive Landscape, §3.2]].

## 1. Case Study: Precision Aerospace Logistics Match

### Candidate Input (Narrational Register)
*   *Profile*: CANDIDATE-LOG-802
*   *Source Narrative*:
    > *"Every autumn, I coordinate our community's meat harvest and distribution. I manage the hunting teams, allocate fuel and supply budgets, set up regional processing hubs, and ensure that elders receive their shares first according to their needs. We trace and document all packaging to prevent spoilage."*

### Employer Operational Requirement (Expository Register)
*   *Employer*: APEX-AERO-01 (Aerospace Component Manufacturer)
*   *Standard Job Requirement*: Warehouse Logistics Lead. Requires ISO-9001 inventory tracking, lot-code tracing, supply allocation management, and coordination of multi-shift dispatch teams.

### System Vector Translation & Blended Match Story
*   *Cosolvent Translation*: The candidate's harvest coordination is identified as a high-dimension match for **Lot-traceable multi-node logistics coordination** and **stakeholder priority allocation**.
*   *Bicultural Story Generation*:
    *   *To Employer*: Shows how the candidate's experience managing food safety tracing and multi-team harvest logistics translates directly into maintaining APEX-AERO-01's zero-defect shipping standards.
    *   *To Candidate*: Tells how APEX-AERO-01's warehouse team has a structured buddy system and supports personal leaves for winter ceremonies.

## 2. Case Study: Precision CNC Machining & Safety Stewardship

### Candidate Input (Narrational Register)
*   *Profile*: CANDIDATE-MACH-405
*   *Source Narrative*:
    > *"I spent three seasons working with our community guardians monitoring salmon weirs and measuring water flows. If the weir obstructed the fish, we had to adjust the gates immediately to preserve the stock. We had to keep accurate weather logs and inspect our gear every morning before going on the water."*

### Employer Operational Requirement (Expository Register)
*   *Employer*: BOREAL-FAB-03 (Precision Precision Fabrication)
*   *Standard Job Requirement*: CNC Machine Operator. Requires strict adherence to preventive maintenance checklists, real-time tool wear adjustment, and compliance with provincial safety standards.

### System Vector Translation & Blended Match Story
*   *Cosolvent Translation*: The guardian weir work maps directly onto **continuous process monitoring** and **preventive maintenance loop compliance**.
*   *Bicultural Story Generation*:
    *   *To Employer*: Highlights the candidate's proven record of executing safety checks under variable field conditions and making precise adjustments to physical barriers.
    *   *To Candidate*: Outlines BOREAL-FAB-03's commitment to mechanical precision and safety, highlighting their flexible shift scheduling.
