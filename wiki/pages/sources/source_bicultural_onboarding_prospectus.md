# source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus

This page serves as a citation target for the business prospectus *Business Prospectus: StoryBoard Integration Systems* (§1).

## Key Claims

- **Expository Register Friction**: Standard technical operating procedures (SOPs) are written in a rules-first, expository register that causes cognitive bandwidth constraints and alienation for Indigenous workers, resulting in high turnover and quality anomalies (§1.1).
- **Relational Storytelling Onboarding solution**: StoryBoard translates expository SOP instructions into a Dual-Register Playbook that pairs the rigid auditable standards with relational, consequence-based storytelling registers modeled on First Nations pedagogy (§1.3).
- **Indigenous Procurement Mandate Mapping**: Direct connection to the federal 5% Indigenous procurement mandate for contractors and TRC Call to Action 92 (§1.2).
- **Decoupled Technology Integration**: Platform builds on the open-source MarketForge stack, utilizing Cosolvent for double-blind matching/narratives and CommonContext for OCAP compliance and local sovereignty (§3.1-§3.2).
- **Workplace Digital Twins**: Uses ClientSynth simulation tools to test candidate matching and schedule flexibility math prior to live funding to prove risk mitigation to corporate sponsors (§3.3).
- **Target Market Segment**: SMEs in high-reliability safety-critical operations (like aerospace components and precision machining) facing target mandates (§4.1).
- **Tripartite Revenue Model**: SOP audits and playbook development ($25,000–$75,000 up-front), SaaS engine licensing ($1,200–$3,500/month), and shared facilitator placement service fees (15% of wages) (§4.2).
- **Non-Dilutive Academic & Impact Incubation**: Leverages Mitacs/SSHRC co-op grants and NACCA/BDC lending to launch as a 51% First Nations joint venture to leverage PSAB contracting rules (§4.3).
