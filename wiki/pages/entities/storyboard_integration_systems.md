# storyboard_integration_systems

**StoryBoard Integration Systems** (StoryBoard) is a bicultural process design and onboarding platform that translates complex Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) into Dual-Register Playbooks, bridging Western industrial safety compliance with First Nations storytelling methodologies [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L27-L30|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §1]].

## 1. Core Service Architecture

StoryBoard provides three layers of operational services to high-reliability B2B suppliers:

### 1.1 SOP Auditing & Process Analysis
StoryBoard audits technical manuals (such as ISO-9001 or AS-9100 directories) to isolate high-friction operations where compliance relies heavily on employee self-discipline (e.g. tool accountability, lock-out tag-out safety loops, and quality checks) [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L53-L55|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §2.1]].

### 1.2 Dual-Register Playbooks
Technical manuals are rewritten by AI Input Translation models (overseen by local Knowledge Keepers) into a paired register:
*   **The Expository Register**: The strict, auditable compliance checklist.
*   **The Narrative Register**: A relational story that explains the consequence and communal duty of the task, withholding explicit moral declarations to prompt internal reflection [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L56-L60|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §2.1]].

### 1.3 Shared Sovereign Facilitators
To lower the overhead of SMEs, StoryBoard pools community-based Elders, coaches, and HR experts. Facilitators guide matched candidates through playbooks and act as mediation layers for operational disputes [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L61-L63|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §2.1]].

## 2. Competitive Differentiation

StoryBoard targets the whitespace between general cultural competence training and rigid quality audits in the Canadian market [[wiki/pages/sources/source_storyboard_competitive_landscape#L49-L51|Source Competitive Landscape, §3]]:

*   **Task-Level Focus**: Unlike platforms like *San'yas* which focus on general attitude awareness, StoryBoard restructures the onboarding instructions and shop-floor SOPs directly [[wiki/pages/sources/source_storyboard_competitive_landscape#L79-L83|Source Competitive Landscape, §4]].
*   **Consulting Scalability**: StoryBoard automates the heavy lifting of manual rewrites via AI-driven templates, lowering costs for SMEs [[wiki/pages/sources/source_storyboard_competitive_landscape#L84-L84|Source Competitive Landscape, §4]].
*   **Execution vs. HR Policy**: Standard toolkits accommodate workers via HR policies (bereavement leave, transport), but StoryBoard guides shop-floor process execution and compliance [[wiki/pages/sources/source_storyboard_competitive_landscape#L35-L39|Source Competitive Landscape, §2.3]].

## 3. Technology Stack Integration

The platform is integrated with the open-source **MarketForge** stack:
*   [[wiki/pages/concepts/bicultural_integration_exchange|Cosolvent Matching Core]]: Manages capability matching and consent-gated double-blind match stories [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L90-L94|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §3.1]].
*   **CommonContext**: Secures data hosting on community-owned local nodes (enforcing OCAP) and runs protocol validations (such as seasonal harvest or ceremony calendar constraints) [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L95-L99|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §3.2]].
*   **ClientSynth**: Generates workplace "digital twin" simulations to prove that flexible scheduling and relational SOPs mitigate turnover risk before companies commit hiring capital [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L100-L104|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §3.3]].

## 4. Commercial Business Model

StoryBoard operates as a 51% Indigenous-owned Joint Venture to utilize Procurement Strategy for Aboriginal Business (PSAB) advantages [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L127-L128|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §4.3]]. It generates revenue through three channels [[wiki/pages/sources/source_bicultural_onboarding_prospectus#L114-L122|Source Onboarding Prospectus, §4.2]]:
1.  **Up-front Auditing/Playbook Contracts**: $25k to $75k per playbook.
2.  **SaaS Retainers**: $1.2k to $3.5k per month for platform access.
3.  **Facilitator Service Placement Fees**: 15% on placement wages.
